# ⏱ 0:00 – 0:30 | The Problem

Hello everyone!

Picture this: it's 2 a.m., you've just had a brilliant idea, and you suddenly decide, *"I'm going to start an AI club!"*

Fast forward to the next morning, and you're staring at a blank screen wondering:

*"Okay... but how do I actually start a community?"*

That's the problem we're solving.

Students have amazing ideas every day, but turning those ideas into reality is hard. There's too much information, too many opinions online, and no clear starting point.

People don't struggle because they lack motivation—they struggle because they don't know what their next step should be.

---

# ⏱ 0:30 – 1:30 | What Our AI Does

That's where our solution comes in.

We built an AI-powered Zero-to-One Builder that helps students move from *"I have an idea"* to *"I know exactly what to do next."*

Let's say a student wants to create a community for people who are just getting started with AI.

Instead of instantly generating a giant to-do list, our AI takes a smarter approach. It starts by asking a few important questions:

• Who is your target audience?

• What problem are you trying to solve?

• What resources do you already have?

• How much time can you realistically dedicate?

Then, instead of pretending there's one perfect answer, the AI helps users understand the tradeoffs behind different choices.

Should they start a Discord server?

A school club?

An Instagram page?

Each option comes with its own benefits and challenges, and our AI clearly explains those differences.

Finally, it generates a practical roadmap with milestones, achievable goals, and most importantly, a clear first step the user can take immediately.

---

# ⏱ 1:30 – 2:00 | The Impact

This is where the real difference happens.

Before using our tool, a student has:

• A great idea

• Lots of enthusiasm

• Absolutely no clue where to begin

After using our tool, they have:

• A clearly defined audience

• An understanding of potential risks

• A realistic 90-day roadmap

• One concrete action they can take today

In short, we're helping students move from overthinking to actually building.

---

# ⏱ 2:00 – 2:30 | Responsible AI

Of course, we know AI isn't magic.

One risk is that users might assume AI recommendations are automatically the correct answer.

To address this, our system presents multiple options, explains the reasoning behind each recommendation, and highlights uncertainty whenever it exists.

Most importantly, the AI never makes the final decision.

Goals, priorities, and personal circumstances are unique to every individual, so the user always remains in control.
